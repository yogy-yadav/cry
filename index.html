Our Legacy

Welcome to one of the most famous milk shops of Hyderabad. Chattu Ram Yadav Milk shop had been established since 1940's by Late Shri Chattu Ram Yadav and been one of the leading sellers of milk products in city of pearls for the past 80 years. Our uniqueness lies in the 'Malai' we serve and is unmatchable. It is prepared and processed with special techniques because of which our shop holds a special place in the heart of the people of Hyderabad. We do also serve other dairy products like Milk, Curd, Butter, Lassi, Rabadi, Bun Maska, Bun Malai and Cream Bun.
We deliver hygiene and high quality milk products to our customers keeping their health as top priority. These products are rich in protein and can be considered as natural supplement.

About the founder

Late Shri Chattu Ram Yadav is the founder of the Chattu Ram Yadav Milk shop. In early 1940's, with clear mindset and lots of effort he started this shop to serve the customers.
